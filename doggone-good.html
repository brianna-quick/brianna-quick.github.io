Doggone Good. Treat Truck

Case Study/Branding

Client

Doggone Good. Treat Truck

Description

Doggone Treat Truck is street food for dogs! Featuring mini dog-safe versions of your favorite foods for your bestie. This campaign’s purpose is to increase awareness and recognition of Doggone’s branding and inform customers of the products they offer. I used Illustrator to create finalized versions of Doggone Treat Truck’s logo. Artwork for the truck wrap and to-go cup were created in Illustrator and a mockup was created to demonstrate the truck wrap in use. Lastly, the menu was created using InDesign and all are displayed using mockups in Photoshop.

Design Brief

Create deliverables to increase awareness and recognition of Doggone’s branding and inform customers of the products they offer.

Deliverables

  • Branding
  • Logo
  • Food Truck Wrap
  • Food Truck Menu
  • To-Go Cup

Target Audience

Dog owners who are worried about their dog's health, but understand that man's best friend needs a little indulgence every now and then.
