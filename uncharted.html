Uncharted

Composite Movie Poster

Client

Uncharted

Description

In Uncharted, sheltered teens leave the futuristic city of Zinhon, where structure is of utmost importance and all live in harmony, for adventure. Reality sets in when they return and find they are no longer recognized as citizens, and now known as “uncharted”. Can they survive the harsh reality of a galaxy unfamiliar to them in search of a new home? Can they ever return? The first poster was created by first researching the images available to determine the composition and then I drew sketches based on these images. After I downloaded the needed images, I masked away the unwanted parts and combined and layered them with other images, effects, and text to create the composite poster. The other two posters were created using this same process.
